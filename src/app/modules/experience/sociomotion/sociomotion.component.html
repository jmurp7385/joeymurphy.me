Sociomotion HackState - Fall 2017 Inspiration Can you systematically gauge how anger someone is based off their posts online? How about sadness? Joy? Disgust? What about their extroversion, or how confident they are? What it does Sociomotion is a web-based application using to analyze their behavior by reading their tweets on Twitter. It uses IBM Watson's Tone Analyzer API, Twitter's API to retrieve the tweets of a user, Node.js, Pug.js (which fully integrates with Express.js, as a supported view engine), Heroku, CSS, and HTML.
